
BLOCK_A(x)

BLOCK_B(x)
1 byte specifier, followed by x bytes of data

\x18: BLOCK(1) as DISPLAY_WORD
\x13: 1 byte UNKNOWN
\xC7: no data UNKNOWN
\x50: 1 byte specifier, followed by BLOCK_A(1)
\x60: 1 byte specifier, followed by BLOCK_A(2)
\x4N: BLOCK_B(N+1)
SPECIFIERS:
\x1b: PHONETICS
